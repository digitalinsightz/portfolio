About Us - Going From "Brick" to "Click"

When the world was hit with unforeseen pandemic, Covid-19, we saw thousands upon thousands of small businesses shut down. Several of these thousands of businesses did not have the means of transferring their business to an online platform or had no idea how. Lots of local businesses know they need to go digital, but do not know where to start.

At Digital Insightz, we want to help guide you starting from a simple path: building a website. Even if there is not much content to promote, we feel that every business needs a website to be referred to, especially by a powerhouse such as Google. If you are lost or simply do not have the time to dive into the digital world, look no further. Our company will make the process of going from brick to click with ease. Our mission is get your business noticed and for new customers to check out what you have to for them to buy.

Please let us know how we can help!
